
1.参考译文 19 Sold out

“剧马上就要开演了，”我说。

“也许已经开演了呢，”苏珊回答说。

我匆匆赶到售票处，问：“我可以买两张票吗？”

“对不起，票已售完。”那位姑娘说。

“真可惜！”苏珊大声说。

正在这时，一个男子匆匆奔向售票处。

“我可以退掉这两张票吗？”他问。

“当然可以，”那姑娘说。

我马上又回到售票处。

“我可以买那两张票吗？”我问。

“当然可以，不过这两张票是下星期三的，您是否还要呢？”

“我还是买下的好，”我垂头丧气地说。

2.中译英

"The play may begin at any moment" I said.

"It may have begun already" Susan said.

I hurried to the ticket office, "Can I have two tickets please?" I asked.

"Sorry, we have sold out" the girl said.

"What's a pity" Susan exclaimed.

Just then, a man hurried to the ticket office.

"May I return these two tickets" he asked.

"Certainly" the girl said.

I went back to the ticket office at once.

"Can I have those two tickets" I asked.

"Certainly, but these two tickets are for next Wednesday's performance. Do you still want them?"

"I might as well have them" I said sadly.
