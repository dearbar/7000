
1.参考译文 17 Always young

我的姑姑詹妮弗是位演员，她至少也有35岁了。尽管如此，她却常在舞台上扮演小姑娘。
詹妮弗很快又要参加一个新剧的演出。这一次，她将扮演一个17岁的少女。
演出时她必须穿一条鲜红色的裙子和黑色的长筒袜。
去年在演另一个剧时，她不得不穿短袜和一件鲜艳的橘红色的衣服。
一旦有人问起她有多大年纪，她总是回答：“亲爱的，长成大人真可怕啊！”

2.中译英

My aunt Jennifer is an actress, she must be at least 35 years old. 
In spite of this, she ofter appears on the stage as a young girl.
Jennifer will take part in a new play soon. This time, she will be a young girl of 17 years old.
She must wear a bright red dress and long black stockings.
Last year in another play, she had to wear short stocks and a bright red dress.
If anyone ever asks her how old she is, she always answer: "Darling, it must be terrible to be grown up!"
